# Alexander & Blake — LinkedIn Company Page Overview

Alexander & Blake is a specialist R&D tax credit advisory firm serving innovative South Carolina businesses.

We work with companies whose teams are building, improving, testing, and solving hard technical problems — often without realizing they may qualify for meaningful federal and state tax incentives. Our role is to translate that innovation into a well-supported credit study: technically grounded, clearly documented, and prepared for long-term reliability.

Our structured engagement process covers the full scope of a credit study — from initial eligibility assessment and technical interviews through QRE analysis, project narratives, methodology documentation, and coordinated CPA filing support. Every deliverable is prepared with the expectation that it may be examined.

We serve manufacturers, software companies, engineering firms, construction businesses, food and beverage producers, and life sciences organizations across the Upstate, Midlands, and Lowcountry. Our team identifies both federal and South Carolina state R&D credits through a single, coordinated engagement.

How we work:
— Complimentary 30-minute eligibility consultation
— Dedicated analyst assigned to every engagement
— Secure client portal for document upload and progress tracking
— Complete credit study with project narratives and Form 6765 support
— Direct CPA coordination for seamless filing
— Full audit defense included at no additional cost
— Contingency fee structure — no credit, no fee

If your company is investing in technical development, product improvement, engineering, or process innovation, we can help you evaluate whether meaningful credits may be available.
